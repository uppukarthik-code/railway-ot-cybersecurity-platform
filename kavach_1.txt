SYSTEM_NAME: Kavach Automatic Train Protection System
DOMAIN: Railway Signalling and Train Protection
STANDARD_REFERENCES:
  - IEC 62443
  - EN 50126
  - EN 50128
  - EN 50129
  - TS 50701
  - IRS Specifications
  - RDSO Kavach Specifications

DESCRIPTION:
Kavach is an indigenous Indian railway Automatic Train Protection (ATP)
system designed to prevent train collisions, overspeeding, signal passing
at danger (SPAD), and unsafe train movements.

Kavach continuously supervises train movement by exchanging information
between onboard equipment, trackside systems, radio infrastructure,
and signalling systems.

The system enforces safe train operation through automatic braking,
movement authority supervision, speed monitoring, and route protection.
