A power utility substation network connecting corporate IT systems to operational
technology (OT) equipment in the field.

The corporate office runs standard IT infrastructure including workstations and
servers on the enterprise network. There is a DMZ separating IT from OT systems.

The OT side includes a SCADA server in the supervisory zone, two Programmable
Logic Controllers (PLCs) managing breaker operations, and four Remote Terminal
Units (RTUs) in the field collecting sensor data. A Human-Machine Interface (HMI)
sits in the control zone for operator access. An OT data historian bridges the
supervisory and DMZ zones for reporting.

All OT-to-OT communication uses IEC 61850 GOOSE messaging. Cross-zone links
are encrypted. The PLCs have redundant peers for high availability.
